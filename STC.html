RPO

With the competition for skilled talent intensifying, it is critical that companies adopt innovative solutions for the identification, attraction and retention of permanent staff. These solutions must deliver visibility and control, greater access to higher quality candidates, process compliance, efficiency and cost savings. There are several benefits from engaging in Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO):
  • • Dedicated and accountable team
  • • Visibility and control over all recruitment activity
  • • Enhanced recruitment processes and compliance
  • • Consistent brand promotion and candidate experience
  • • Effective careers site, direct hire and supplier sourcing channels
  • • Greater choice and enhanced quality of candidates
  • • Reduced HR and Manager time
  • • Process efficiency delivering reduced time to hire
  • • Controlled and reduced cost per hire
  • • A scalable solution that aligns with your needs
We in Staffind can be your optimum solution on RPO.
